Motion principles
Scroll-pinned feature reveals — one feature owns the screen at a time Same pattern as alma.food. Each section (Snap & Scan, Cycle Sync, Budget) gets its own full-viewport moment.
Slow, deliberate easing — 600–900ms transitions, cubic-bezier(.22,1,.36,1) Speed communicates confidence. Nothing snaps or bounces.
Text enters before visuals — headline lands first, then the app frame animates in Forces the message to register before the aesthetic takes over.
Nutrient bars count up on scroll entry — never on page load Data should feel earned, not dumped. Animate numbers when they enter the viewport.
